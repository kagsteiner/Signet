Signet

by Karlheinz Agsteiner

A private manuscript space.

Signet is a quiet writing studio for long-form storytelling.
No dashboards.
No toolbars.
No noise.

Just text.

And a single mark at the end of a paragraph.

When you’re stuck, press it.
You’ll receive one continuation sentence.

Undo if it’s not yours.
